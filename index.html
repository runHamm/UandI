Who are we?

Welcome to the U&I Association of friends, a non political, non secretive organization founded in 2011 by a group of friends who came together to support one another in times of need. Our motto, Love your friend as yourself reflects our commitment to promoting unity, understanding, and goodwill amongs our members

Our association is built on the principle of friendship, mutual support, and community service. We aim to provide assistance to our members in various ways including financial support, emotional support and social events.

Over the years we have organized numereous weddings, child dedications, business seminars, and health workshops for our members. We have also received awards from socities and ministries for our contributions to the community.

Our journey has not been without challenges, but we remain grateful for the blessings and opportunities we have received. Join us in our mission to spread love, kindness, and support among our members and the wider community. Together lets make a difference.
